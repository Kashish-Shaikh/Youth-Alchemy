## INGREDIENT: Retinol
Category: Retinoid
Benefits: [collagen_stimulation, cell_turnover, anti_aging, acne_treatment, hyperpigmentation, pore_minimizing]
Best_For: [mature_skin, acne_prone, hyperpigmentation, anti_aging]
Concentration: 0.025% (starter) | 0.1% (low) | 0.3% (medium) | 0.5-1% (high)
Avoid_If: [pregnant, breastfeeding, active_eczema_flare, rosacea_sensitive_subtype, extremely_dry_skin_without_buffer]
Allergens: [rare_retinol_sensitivity]
Contraindications: [pregnancy, breastfeeding, concurrent_waxing_treated_area, laser_recent, benzoyl_peroxide_same_time]
Pregnancy_Safe: FALSE — Avoid all retinoids (retinol, retinal, tretinoin, adapalene, tazarotene)
Interactions: [do_not_layer_with_benzoyl_peroxide, do_not_layer_with_aha_bha_same_night_if_sensitive, vitamin_c_morning_retinol_night_is_fine]
Evidence_Level: High
Usage: PM only. Always use SPF next day. Buffer with moisturizer if new to retinoids.
