# ALLERGIES & SENSITIVITIES KNOWLEDGE BASE
# Module: allergies | Version: 1.0

---

## ALLERGY: Fragrance
Allergy_Ingredients: [fragrance, parfum, linalool, limonene, geraniol, citronellol, eugenol, cinnamal, coumarin, benzyl_alcohol, benzyl_salicylate, isoeugenol, farnesol, cinnamyl_alcohol, oakmoss_extract, treemoss_extract]
Severity: Moderate-High (most common cosmetic allergen)
Safe_Alternatives: [fragrance_free_products, certified_fragrance_free_labeling]
Notes: "Unscented" does not mean fragrance-free — products may contain masking fragrances.

---

## ALLERGY: Essential_Oils
Allergy_Ingredients: [tea_tree_oil, lavender_oil, eucalyptus_oil, peppermint_oil, rosemary_oil, bergamot_oil, lemon_oil, orange_oil, clary_sage, ylang_ylang, frankincense, camphor, menthol]
Severity: Moderate
Safe_Alternatives: [fragrance_free_products, centella_asiatica, aloe_vera, oat_extract]
Notes: Essential oils are common sensitizers. Phototoxic citrus oils cause UV reactions.

---

## ALLERGY: Aspirin_Salicylates
Allergy_Ingredients: [salicylic_acid, salicylates_topical, aspirin, willow_bark_extract]
Severity: Moderate
Safe_Alternatives: [glycolic_acid_aha, lactic_acid, mandelic_acid, azelaic_acid]
Notes: Cross-reactivity with salicylic acid in aspirin-allergic individuals. Use caution.

---

## ALLERGY: Nickel

## ALLERGY: Latex

Safe_Alternatives: [mineral_oil, squalane, sunflower_seed_oil, rice_bran_oil, ceramides]

## ALLERGY: Oat_Gluten

Safe_Alternatives: [centella_asiatica, aloe_vera, ceramides, allantoin]

## ALLERGY: Preservatives

## CONDITION: Pregnancy
Unsafe_Ingredients: [retinol, retinal, tretinoin, adapalene, tazarotene, tazorac, isotretinoin, hydroquinone, high_dose_salicylic_acid, benzoyl_peroxide_high_conc, chemical_peels_high_conc, high_conc_essential_oils, oxybenzone, formaldehyde_releasers, kojic_acid_uncertain]
Safe_Alternatives: [azelaic_acid, vitamin_c_topical, hyaluronic_acid, ceramides, glycerin, niacinamide, alpha_arbutin, tranexamic_acid, mineral_spf_zinc_oxide, lactic_acid_low_conc, glycolic_acid_low_conc, colloidal_oatmeal]
Notes: When in doubt, consult OB-GYN. Topical absorption is lower than oral but still a concern. Safety > cosmetic benefits.
Rule: REMOVE all retinoids AND hydroquinone. FLAG chemical sunscreen filters (prefer mineral). CAUTION on high-dose BHA/AHA.

---

## CONDITION: Breastfeeding
Unsafe_Ingredients: [retinoids_all_forms, hydroquinone_breast_area, high_conc_salicylic_near_nursing_area]
Safe_Alternatives: [same_as_pregnancy_safe_list]
Notes: Similar caution to pregnancy. Avoid high-concentration actives especially near nursing areas.

---

## CONDITION: Aspirin_Medication
Unsafe_Ingredients: [salicylic_acid_high_dose_topical_systemic_concern]
Notes: Low concentration topical (≤2%) generally safe but flag for awareness.
Rule: FLAG salicylic acid use. Recommend consulting physician for high-concentration use.

---

## CONDITION: Blood_Thinners (Warfarin, Heparin, Aspirin_high_dose)
Unsafe_Ingredients: [high_conc_vitamin_e_oral_not_topical, fish_oil_oral_not_topical]
Notes: Primarily relevant for oral supplements. Topical skincare generally safe but flag.
Rule: FLAG vitamin K in skincare if on warfarin (theoretical interaction). Consult physician.

---

## CONDITION: Kidney_Disease
Unsafe_Ingredients: [high_dose_topical_salicylic_acid_systemic_absorption_risk]
Notes: BHA at high concentrations over large body surface areas may have systemic concern.
Rule: FLAG high-concentration salicylic acid. Recommend physician consultation.

---

## CONDITION: Immunosuppression (Transplant, Autoimmune_Meds)
Notes: Increased infection risk from disrupted barrier. Avoid ingredients that cause significant barrier disruption without medical supervision.
Rule: FLAG strong exfoliants and retinoids without physician clearance.

---

## CONDITION: Photosensitive_Medications (Tetracyclines, Fluoroquinolones, Thiazides, Amiodarone, St_Johns_Wort)
Unsafe_Ingredients: [aha_glycolic_without_mandatory_spf, bha_without_spf, retinoids_without_spf, bergamot_oil_phototoxic, lime_oil_phototoxic]
Notes: Medications cause skin photosensitivity. All exfoliants become higher risk.
Rule: MANDATORY SPF50. FLAG all exfoliants — recommend AM use avoided or strict SPF adherence.

---

## CONDITION: Isotretinoin_Current_or_Recent (Accutane)
Unsafe_Ingredients: [waxing_treated_areas, laser_treated_areas, strong_chemical_peels, retinoids_topical_concurrent, high_conc_aha_bha]
Notes: Skin extremely fragile on isotretinoin. Minimal routine only.
Rule: Remove all exfoliants, retinoids, vitamin C high conc. Barrier repair only.
