# SKIN TYPES KNOWLEDGE BASE
# Module: skin_types | Version: 1.0 | Evidence_Level: Established

---

## SKIN_TYPE: Normal
Characteristics: Balanced sebum production, minimal pores, no excessive shine or dryness, even skin tone, rarely breaks out
Best_For: [hyaluronic_acid, niacinamide, peptides, vitamin_c, retinol, aha_bha, ceramides, antioxidants]
Avoid_If: []
Recommended_Routine_AM: [gentle_cleanser, vitamin_c_serum, moisturizer, spf]
Recommended_Routine_PM: [gentle_cleanser, retinol_or_peptides, moisturizer]
Notes: Most ingredients are well-tolerated. Focus on maintenance and prevention.

---

## SKIN_TYPE: Oily
Characteristics: Excess sebum, enlarged pores, shiny appearance especially T-zone, prone to blackheads and acne, thicker skin texture
Best_For: [niacinamide, salicylic_acid, zinc, retinol, aha, clay, benzoyl_peroxide, green_tea_extract, hyaluronic_acid_lightweight]
Avoid_If: [heavy_oils, petrolatum, coconut_oil, shea_butter_heavy, mineral_oil, heavy_occlusive_creams]
Recommended_Routine_AM: [foaming_cleanser, niacinamide_serum, lightweight_gel_moisturizer, oil_free_spf]
Recommended_Routine_PM: [foaming_cleanser, bha_exfoliant, niacinamide_or_retinol, lightweight_moisturizer]
Notes: Avoid over-stripping — this triggers more sebum. Hydration is still essential.

---

## SKIN_TYPE: Dry
Characteristics: Tight feeling after cleansing, flakiness, rough texture, dull appearance, fine lines more visible, low sebum
Best_For: [hyaluronic_acid, ceramides, squalane, glycerin, shea_butter, fatty_acids, peptides, niacinamide, oils_rich_in_linoleic_acid]
Avoid_If: [alcohol_denat, high_concentration_aha, benzoyl_peroxide_high_strength, harsh_sulfates, acetone, strong_retinoids_without_buffer]
Recommended_Routine_AM: [cream_cleanser, hydrating_toner, hyaluronic_acid_serum, rich_moisturizer, spf]
Recommended_Routine_PM: [cream_cleanser, facial_oil_or_serum, rich_moisturizer, occlusives_optional]
Notes: Layer hydration. Apply moisturizer on damp skin to lock in water.

---

## SKIN_TYPE: Combination
Characteristics: Oily T-zone (forehead, nose, chin), dry or normal cheeks, enlarged pores in T-zone, occasional breakouts on nose/chin
Best_For: [niacinamide, hyaluronic_acid, lightweight_moisturizers, gentle_aha, vitamin_c, ceramides, gentle_retinol]
Avoid_If: [heavy_occlusive_on_tzone, strong_drying_agents_all_over, very_heavy_creams]
Recommended_Routine_AM: [gentle_cleanser, niacinamide_serum, lightweight_moisturizer, spf]
Recommended_Routine_PM: [gentle_cleanser, aha_on_oily_zones, retinol_or_peptides, lightweight_moisturizer]
Notes: Zone treatment is effective — apply richer products only to dry areas.

---

## SKIN_TYPE: Sensitive
Characteristics: Easily irritated by products, redness, stinging, burning sensation, reactive to weather and environmental factors, may have rosacea tendency
Best_For: [centella_asiatica, aloe_vera, ceramides, niacinamide_low_concentration, peptides, hyaluronic_acid, azelaic_acid_low, oat_extract, allantoin, bisabolol]
Avoid_If: [fragrance, essential_oils, alcohol_denat, high_concentration_vitamin_c, strong_acids_high_ph, benzoyl_peroxide, strong_retinoids, witch_hazel_high_alcohol, menthol, eucalyptus, camphor, sodium_lauryl_sulfate]
Recommended_Routine_AM: [fragrance_free_gentle_cleanser, soothing_serum, barrier_repair_moisturizer, mineral_spf]
Recommended_Routine_PM: [micellar_water_or_gentle_cleanser, calming_serum, rich_barrier_cream]
Notes: Always patch test. Introduce one new product at a time. Fewer ingredients = lower risk.

---

## SKIN_TYPE: Acne_Prone
Characteristics: Regular breakouts (comedones, papules, pustules), may overlap with oily skin, enlarged pores, post-acne marks
Best_For: [salicylic_acid, benzoyl_peroxide, niacinamide, retinol, azelaic_acid, tea_tree_oil, zinc, adapalene, aha_for_exfoliation]
Avoid_If: [coconut_oil, algae_extract_high, isopropyl_myristate, sodium_lauryl_sulfate, heavy_occlusive, comedogenic_ingredients]
Recommended_Routine_AM: [bha_cleanser_or_gentle_cleanser, niacinamide_serum, oil_free_moisturizer, non_comedogenic_spf]
Recommended_Routine_PM: [bha_cleanser, benzoyl_peroxide_spot_or_retinol, niacinamide, oil_free_moisturizer]
Notes: Do not use BHA and benzoyl peroxide simultaneously — layer on alternate nights or use BP in AM only.

---

## SKIN_TYPE: Mature
Characteristics: Fine lines and wrinkles, loss of firmness, dullness, thinner skin, age spots, decreased moisture retention
Best_For: [retinol_high_strength, peptides, vitamin_c, hyaluronic_acid, niacinamide, ceramides, coenzyme_q10, resveratrol, bakuchiol, glycolic_acid]
Avoid_If: [harsh_stripping_cleansers, excessive_alcohol, high_fragrance]
Recommended_Routine_AM: [antioxidant_cleanser, vitamin_c_serum, peptide_moisturizer, broad_spectrum_spf50]
Recommended_Routine_PM: [gentle_cleanser, retinol_high, rich_peptide_cream, facial_oil_optional]
Notes: SPF is the most evidence-backed anti-aging intervention. Consistent retinol use is second.
