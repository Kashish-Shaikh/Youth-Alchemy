## CONCERN: Acne_Vulgaris
Symptoms: Blackheads, whiteheads, papules, pustules, nodules, cysts
Causes: Excess sebum, C. acnes bacteria, follicular hyperkeratinization, inflammation
Severity: mild | moderate | severe
Evidence_Based_Treatments:
  mild: [salicylic_acid_0.5_2%, benzoyl_peroxide_2.5_5%, retinoids_otc, niacinamide_5_10%, azelaic_acid_10_20%]
  moderate: [adapalene_0.1_0.3%, benzoyl_peroxide_5_10%, combination_bha_retinoid, topical_antibiotics_rx_only]
  severe: [isotretinoin_rx_only, oral_antibiotics_rx_only, refer_dermatologist]
Ingredients_Best_For: [salicylic_acid, benzoyl_peroxide, adapalene, retinol, niacinamide, azelaic_acid, zinc, tea_tree_oil]
Ingredients_Avoid: [heavy_oils, comedogenic_ingredients, heavy_occlusive, isopropyl_myristate]
Evidence_Level: High
Notes: Combination of BHA + retinoid is highly effective but start slow to avoid irritation.

## CONCERN: Hyperpigmentation
Symptoms: Dark spots, uneven skin tone, post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH), melasma
Causes: UV damage, hormonal changes (melasma), post-acne marks, inflammation
Severity: mild | moderate | severe
Evidence_Based_Treatments:
  mild: [vitamin_c_10_20%, niacinamide_5_10%, azelaic_acid_10_20%, alpha_arbutin, kojic_acid, aha_glycolic]
  moderate: [hydroquinone_2_4%, tranexamic_acid, retinoids, combination_regimens]
  severe: [hydroquinone_4%_rx, chemical_peels_professional, laser_treatment, refer_dermatologist]
Ingredients_Best_For: [vitamin_c, niacinamide, azelaic_acid, alpha_arbutin, kojic_acid, tranexamic_acid, glycolic_acid, retinol, licorice_root]
Ingredients_Avoid: [fragrance_triggers_PIH, phototoxic_ingredients_without_spf]
Evidence_Level: High
Notes: SPF is mandatory — all brightening treatments are futile without daily sun protection.

## CONCERN: Rosacea
Symptoms: Persistent redness, visible blood vessels (telangiectasia), flushing, papules, pustules (acne rosacea subtype)
Causes: Genetic predisposition, Demodex mites, vascular dysfunction, environmental triggers (heat, alcohol, spicy food)
Severity: mild | moderate | severe
Evidence_Based_Treatments:
  mild: [azelaic_acid_10_15%, niacinamide_4%, green_tea_extract, centella_asiatica, soothing_barrier_repair]
  moderate: [metronidazole_rx, ivermectin_rx, azelaic_acid_15_20%]
  severe: [oral_antibiotics_rx, laser_rx, refer_dermatologist]
Ingredients_Best_For: [azelaic_acid, niacinamide, centella_asiatica, green_tea_extract, ceramides, aloe_vera, allantoin]
Ingredients_Avoid: [alcohol_denat, fragrance, essential_oils, menthol, witch_hazel, high_acids, benzoyl_peroxide, strong_retinoids, physical_exfoliants]
Evidence_Level: High
Notes: Avoid all known personal triggers. Mineral SPF (zinc oxide) preferred over chemical filters.

## CONCERN: Eczema_Atopic_Dermatitis
Symptoms: Itching, dry patches, inflamed red skin, scaling, weeping lesions in flares
Causes: Impaired skin barrier, immune dysregulation, allergen sensitivity, environmental triggers
Severity: mild | moderate | severe
Evidence_Based_Treatments:
  mild: [ceramides_moisturizer, colloidal_oatmeal, fragrance_free_products, gentle_cleansers]
  moderate: [topical_corticosteroids_rx, tacrolimus_rx, barrier_repair_intensives]
  severe: [dupilumab_biologic_rx, systemic_immunosuppressants_rx, refer_dermatologist]
Ingredients_Best_For: [ceramides, hyaluronic_acid, colloidal_oatmeal, shea_butter, glycerin, allantoin, centella_asiatica, petrolatum]
Ingredients_Avoid: [fragrance, essential_oils, sodium_lauryl_sulfate, alcohol_denat, preservatives_methylisothiazolinone, dyes, high_acids]
Evidence_Level: High
Allergens_Flag: High_sensitivity_check_required
Notes: Always use fragrance-free, dye-free. Wet wrap therapy effective in severe flares.

## CONCERN: Anti_Aging
Symptoms: Fine lines, wrinkles, loss of firmness, dullness, thinning skin, age spots
Causes: Intrinsic aging, UV photodamage, glycation, oxidative stress, collagen/elastin degradation
Severity: mild | moderate | advanced
Evidence_Based_Treatments:
  mild: [spf_daily, vitamin_c_serum, gentle_retinoid, peptides, antioxidants]
  moderate: [retinol_0.5_1%, glycolic_acid_aha, vitamin_c_high_concentration, peptide_complexes, hyaluronic_acid]
  advanced: [tretinoin_rx, prescription_retinoids, professional_treatments, refer_dermatologist]
Ingredients_Best_For: [retinol, retinal, tretinoin, vitamin_c, peptides, glycolic_acid, hyaluronic_acid, niacinamide, coq10, resveratrol, bakuchiol]
Ingredients_Avoid: [high_fragrance, oxidizing_formulations]
Evidence_Level: High
Notes: Retinoids (prescription or OTC) and daily SPF50+ are the gold-standard interventions.

## CONCERN: Dehydration
Symptoms: Tight feeling, dullness, fine lines that improve with moisture, skin feels rough
Causes: Inadequate water intake, low humidity, over-cleansing, disrupted barrier, harsh actives
Severity: mild | moderate
Evidence_Based_Treatments:
  mild: [hyaluronic_acid_serum, glycerin_based_moisturizer, occlusives, humectants]
  moderate: [layered_hydration_routine, barrier_repair_ingredients, reduce_harsh_actives]
Ingredients_Best_For: [hyaluronic_acid, glycerin, sodium_pca, beta_glucan, aloe_vera, ceramides, squalane]
Ingredients_Avoid: [alcohol_denat, over_exfoliation, harsh_sulfates]
Evidence_Level: Established
Notes: Dehydration is a condition, not a skin type — oily skin can be dehydrated.

## CONCERN: Perioral_Dermatitis
Symptoms: Small red papules and pustules around mouth, nose, and eyes; can be itchy or burning
Causes: Topical corticosteroids, fluorinated toothpaste, heavy occlusive creams, hormonal
Severity: mild | moderate
Evidence_Based_Treatments:
  mild: [stop_topical_steroids, avoid_heavy_creams, azelaic_acid, niacinamide]
  moderate: [topical_metronidazole_rx, oral_antibiotics_rx, refer_dermatologist]
Ingredients_Best_For: [azelaic_acid, niacinamide, minimal_gentle_products]
Ingredients_Avoid: [topical_corticosteroids, heavy_occlusive_creams, fluorinated_toothpaste_adjacent, fragrance]
Evidence_Level: Established

## CONCERN: Dark_Circles
Symptoms: Discoloration under eyes, blue/purple or brown tones, hollowness (structural)
Causes: Vascular (blue/purple), pigmentation (brown), structural/volume loss, fatigue, genetics
Severity: cosmetic
Evidence_Based_Treatments:
  topical: [vitamin_c_eye_cream, caffeine, retinol_low, vitamin_k, peptides]
  medical: [filler_rx, laser_rx, refer_dermatologist_if_structural]
Ingredients_Best_For: [caffeine, vitamin_c, retinol_low, vitamin_k, peptides, hyaluronic_acid]
Ingredients_Avoid: [high_concentration_retinol_under_eyes, fragrance_near_eyes, high_acids_near_eyes]
Evidence_Level: Moderate
